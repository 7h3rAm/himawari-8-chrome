Here you can choose the image source.

The Japanese Himawari 8 Satellite takes images of different parts of the spectrum (visible light and infrared). Himawari offers the best image quality but the satellite is geo stationary.

You can also choose images from the NASA's EPIC camera on board of the DSCOVR satellite. This satellite always faces the sunlit sunlit side of the Earth.

The NOAA GOES satellites are geo stationary like Himawari but show the pacific (GOES West) and the Americas (GOES East).
